Our Services

Consultations

We are always available to offer advice, but if you would like someone’s undivided attention we are pleased to offer an in-store consultation service as well as an at home consultation service. Did you know that Russell Nursery is the authorized BC distributor for Halls English Greenhouses? We have a number of models set up at the nursery but if you aren’t sure which one is right for you we also offer a greenhouse consultation at your home.

Landscapers

Revitalize your garden with Outback Nursery's expert landscaper consultations. We specialize in tailoring designs to your unique space and preferences, ensuring a sustainable, vibrant outdoor area. Our process includes a thorough site evaluation, personalized design planning, and detailed execution strategies. Reach out today to begin your garden transformation journey with our skilled team.

Workshops

Our workshop will offer some labour-saving ideas and highlight some of the many materials to consider when building your soil. Participants usually work on preparing and placing materials in order to get the best results and find out what each component adds to the soil. Protection of soil structure and nutrients through the seasons will also be discussed. our workshop is hands-on and involves moving some heavy materials, i.e. composted manure, compost, etc. by shovel and wheelbarrow.

Get Gift Certificates In 3 Easy Steps!

Introduce yourself by sending an email to info@outbacknursery.ca

This step is asking you to initiate contact with Outback Nursery by sending them an email. The email address provided, info@outbacknursery.ca, is where you should send your message. When you send the email, you should introduce yourself, provide any necessary details, and specify the purpose of your communication. This step is essentially the first point of contact with the nursery, allowing them to know who you are and why you are reaching out to them.

Make a quick payment via E-transfer to info@outbacknursery.ca, specifying your chosen amount.

Once you've made contact and established what you want to purchase or the service you're interested in, the next step is to make a payment. In this case, they are asking you to make a payment via E-transfer to the same email address, info@outbacknursery.ca. E-transfer is a secure and convenient way to transfer funds electronically from your bank account to the recipient's account. When making the transfer, you need to specify the amount you are paying, ensuring that it matches the agreed-upon price for the product or service.

Sit back and relax as we prepare your personalized voucher. We'll scan and email it directly to you or to a recipient of your choice.

After you've made the payment, Outback Nursery will process your order or service request. In this step, they are letting you know that they will prepare a personalized voucher for you. This voucher could be for various purposes, such as a gift certificate, discount coupon, or proof of purchase. Once the voucher is ready, they will scan it and send it to you via email. Alternatively, if you're purchasing the voucher as a gift for someone else, they can send it directly to the recipient's email address. This step assures you that once you've completed the payment, they will take care of the rest and ensure you receive your voucher promptly.
